NMS Compendium of Past Questions

The online NMS compendium of past questions is designed to provide helpful resources and guidance to candidates who are interested in pursuing their education through the prestigious Nigerian Military School (NMS).

We understand the application process can be challenging, so our goal is to make it easier by providing tips, sample exam questions with their corresponding answers, as well as general insights into life at NMS.

To begin, you need to sign up and choose your preferred plan, then begin to enjoy access to the resources which will prepare you for success during the national entrance examinations for admission into NMS.
